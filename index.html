Basic Income Action - Seattle

Basic Income is the common-sense and academically supported system in which the government guarantees every citizen, regardless of circumstance, an unconditional amount of money that guarantees basic needs will be met. Read more about it in Wikipedia.

Basic Income Action - Seattle is a Seattle-based group aiming to raise awareness of Basic Income with the aim of implementing this policy nationally in the USA. It's part of the larger Basic Income Action national organization.
