General information

Universal Basic Income is a policy which guarantees every person a regular stipend, no strings attached, to meet their basic survival needs.

It is

  • universal: everyone receives it
  • basic: it is just enough to meet bare survival needs
  • income: it is simply cash in your pocket, no strings attached

UBI has been studied extensively in the past and has been shown to be good for individuals and for society. In today's precarious economy, there is renewed interest in this policy and a new wave of trials is being conducted around the world.

Basic Income Action, Seattle is the Seattle chapter of the national Basic Income Action organization. Our mission is to raise awareness of Universal Basic Income so that the USA decides to implement this policy nationally.
